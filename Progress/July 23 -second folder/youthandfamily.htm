Human Services Division

Youth & Family Division

Economic Development Initiative

The FOCUS Youth and Family Development Programs provide quality educational services for children during afterschool hours, and during the summer months. It offers families with access to information, support and resources that help develop and maximize their strengths and potential. The objective of the division is to provide youth & families with enriching experiences that broaden their perspectives, improve their socialization, and enhance their skills to improve their overall quality of life.
