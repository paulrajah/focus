Family Programs

FOCUS Family Success Center (FFSC)

As one of eleven Newark-based Family Success Centers, the FOCUS Family Success Center provides a comprehensive family support system for families residing in Newark with emphasis on the Central Ward. The FFSC aims to prevent child abuse and neglect by providing families with innovative life skills training, workshops, parent-child activities, and support groups to prevent child-abuse and neglect, and improving the overall quality of life of children.
